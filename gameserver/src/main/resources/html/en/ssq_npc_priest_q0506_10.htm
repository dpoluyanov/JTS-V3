Dawn Priest:
[Seal of Avarice]
The Seal of Avarice bestows the following benefits:
Ownership of the Necropolises: The Lords of Dawn have exclusive rights to the eight Necropolises. In the case of a tie, entry is no longer exclusive.
Meeting with Lilith: The door to the Forbidden Sanctum opens and we finally meet Lilith, the Abyssal Mother. She is our greatest enemy, sleeping in the Saint's Necropolis.
Commerce with Merchants of Mammon:You can do business with Merchants of Mammon in the Necropolises. They accept payment in Ancient Adena, and have many fine, rare articles.
