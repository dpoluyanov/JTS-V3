Mysterious Wizard:
The Dark Arts involve the magical study of the relationships between life and death. They were forbidden by the gods because they fear our practicioners will unlock the secrets known only to the divine...
For our art, we work with materials that have experienced both life and death but which have not yet returned to nature -- corpses and the undecomposed bones of the dead, in other words. These are the essential ingredients of the Dark Arts.
