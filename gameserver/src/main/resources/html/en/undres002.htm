Abyssal Celebrant Undrias:
The Temple of Shilen is the dark heart of our dark race. It is over 2,000 years old, and it has survived untold wars and disasters. It was long hidden from our enemies by a curtain of silvery spider webs spun by Harnea of the Arachne.
Now, though, it stands revealed in all its glory. All Dark Elven younglings here undergo the rites necessary to receive Shilen's blessing before becoming Dark Fighters or Dark Mystics.
