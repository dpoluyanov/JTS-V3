Priest Tanios:
We Elves serve Elvas and Andariel -- Eva and Einhasad, in the Human tongue. Our faith is closely connected to Mother Tree. It is the symbol and archetype of all the trees created by the goddesses, as well as the means by which they communicate their will to us. The Mother Tree here in Innadril was grown from a small branch brought from the Elvin Forest's Mother Tree itself, so the two trees are connected as one living being...
