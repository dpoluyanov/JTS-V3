Register Shortcut
Your battles will be more efficient and exciting if you create shortcuts for skills, actions, equipment, items, etc.
Press Alt + F1-F12 or click the Move button on the shortcut window to move to other shortcut groups. If you have a lot of shortcuts to register, group them together for more efficient and convient play.
Soulshots and Spiritshots will be automatically loaded by pressing a shortcut. Click the right mouse button to cancel this process.
You can extend the shortcut window up to 3 rows by pressing the '<<' key.
