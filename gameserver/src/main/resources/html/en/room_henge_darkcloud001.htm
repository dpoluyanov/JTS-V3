Symbol of Adversity:
I knew you would succeed!
However, there are still two tests left, so be alert -- they will be more challenging than the tests you have already passed.
But first I shall grant you a reward. You have proven your trustworthiness, and as a show of gratitude on behalf of those you have helped, I give you this advice.Go to the Isle of Prayer and find a man named Rignos. Accept his offer, though it may seem strange, and you shall earn a secret key that will prove essential at the Emerald Square.
