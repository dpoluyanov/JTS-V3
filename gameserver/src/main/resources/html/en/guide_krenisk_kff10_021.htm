Newbie Guide:
Weapons are divided into 7 types: One-Handed Sword, Two-Handed Sword, Blunt Weapon, Dagger, Polearm, Fist and Bow. Among those, let's first focus on the two Sword types, Blunt Weapons, Dagger and Bow. If you are a Kamael, listen especially carefully about the One-Handed Sword, Two-Handed Sword, Blunt Weapons and Bow.
