Gabrielle:
Giran is an immensely rich land. She appears bright and vital on the outside, but there are dark undercurrents visible in the eyes of her inhabitants. They live in mortal fear of a tyrant they know as the disaster of Giran, otherwise known as the Land Dragon Antharas. If he stretches out and leaves his lair in Dragon Valley, the prosperity and peace of their land will be irreparably broken.
