Newbie Guide:
You must transfer your class at level 20. If you don't, there is nothing more that you can be taught and no new skills that you can learn.
If you choose to transfer to a Trooper, you may learn unique skills only known to Troopers. For example, if you use an Ancient Sword, you can learn Ancient Sword Mastery, which will raise your attack power when using a Rapier, or Dark Strike, which strikes enemies with a Sword, Ancient Sword, or Blunt Weapon.
How do those sound to you? Now, continue your training and complete your class transfer. Only then you will get what you desire!
