Blacksmith of Mammon:
Ancient Adena was the currency used during the Elmoreden Empire. It was minted from the highest-quality gold, much more pure than today's currency. Legend says that the emblem on the back of the coins possesses a mysterious, magical power...
The Lords of Dawn and Revolutionaries of Dusk are given bonuses in the form of Ancient Adena. They have a particular fondness for seal stone, and will pay you in Ancient Adena for it.
