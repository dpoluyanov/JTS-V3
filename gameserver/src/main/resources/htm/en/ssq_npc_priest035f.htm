Dusk Priestess:
[What are the Seven Signs?]
During the reign of the Elmoreden Empire, seven powerful seals were crafted by Einhasad, the Goddess of Light, to contain Shilen, the Goddess of Death.Two groups, the Rulers of Dawn and the Revolutionaries of Dusk, are now battling for control of the seals. To whom will you pledge your allegiance?
The Rulers of Dawn consist of castle-holding clans or alliances; the Revolutionaries of Dusk consist of everyone else. The struggle for the Seven Signs is ultimately a battle between castle-owners and non-castle owners.
