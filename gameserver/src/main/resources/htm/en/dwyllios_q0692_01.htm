Dilios:
I don't know how a stranger like you came to be here, but you should know that this is a very different place than Aden. Here it is might that makes right.
This has always been a hard and barren land, but since the appearance of so many monsters it has become even worse. Many of the survivors envy the dead their peace.
But for those strong enough to prevail, there is glory and wealth to be had. Of course, very few possess the necessary traits to withstand the crushing darkness. If you can, perhaps there is hope after all...
"I will prove my worth."
