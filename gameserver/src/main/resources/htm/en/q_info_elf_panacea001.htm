Record of the Mother Tree Guardian:
The Elven Secret Hidden by the Guardians of the Mother Tree
The guardians of the Mother Tree do not have an easy task.
The constant tension wears away at their composure, so they hide their feelings behind a mask.
Their frustations manifest themselves in a vicious hatred of all other races. They are hopelessly cynical and pessimistic.
They frequently misunderstand the ritual sacrifice of the life of a guardian.
