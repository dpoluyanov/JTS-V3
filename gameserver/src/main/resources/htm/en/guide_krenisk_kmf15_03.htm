Newbie Guide:
The hunting area I recommend for warriors at your level is the Hills of Gold, located south of the village. If you choose to hunt a Muertos Guard, Muertos Scouts, Muertos Warriors or Colossuses in the Hills of Gold, however, you would be advised to first advance a level.
Once you are fully comfortable in battle, you might also wish to hunt in Nornil's Cave, located far north of the village. Take great care when hunting at Nornil's Cave, though! You may confidently hunt Grotto Golems or Shady Muertos Guards at your current level, but the other monsters there will be beyond your current skill.
